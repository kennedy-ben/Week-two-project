HOW GHANAIAN CULTURE IS UPHELD

This application consists of Akan names which are derived from Ghanaian culture based on the week they were born and the days. Most people know their birthday according to the week or day they were born, often if it was a holiday. However, Ghanaians remember the day according to their week days. For example, if a male is born on Sunday, his name will be 'Kwasi', and if a female is born on Sunday, she will be called 'Akosua'. This is different from other African cultures and also naming children.
